# Countdown timer (Medium)

**Prompt (copy-paste into Vibetree Pro)**

```
Build a countdown timer app with presets. Main screen: large countdown display with circular progress ring. Preset buttons (1 min, 5 min, 10 min, custom). Start/Pause/Reset controls. When timer ends: play sound, show alert, optional vibration. History tab: list of completed timers with duration and date. Settings: choose alarm sound. Persist presets and history with Codable + UserDefaults.
```
