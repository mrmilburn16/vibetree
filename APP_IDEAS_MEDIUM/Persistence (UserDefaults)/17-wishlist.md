# Wishlist (Medium)

**Prompt (copy-paste into Vibetree Pro)**

```
Build a wishlist app with priorities. Main screen: list of items with name, estimated price, and priority tag (Must Have, Nice to Have, Dream). Add/edit sheet: name, price, URL (optional), priority picker, notes. Sort by price or priority. Total estimated cost at top. Swipe to mark as purchased (moves to Purchased tab). Two tabs: Active and Purchased. Persist with Codable + UserDefaults.
```
