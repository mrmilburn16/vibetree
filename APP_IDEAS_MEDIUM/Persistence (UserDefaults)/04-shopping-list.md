```
Build a shopping list app with categories. Main screen: sectioned list grouped by aisle (Produce, Dairy, etc.). Each item has name, quantity, and checkbox. Add item sheet with name, quantity stepper, and category picker. Swipe to delete. Button to clear all checked items with confirmation. Show total items and checked count.
```
