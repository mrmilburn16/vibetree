# Recipe card (Medium)

**Prompt (copy-paste into Vibetree Pro)**

```
Build a recipe book app. Main screen: grid of recipe cards with title and cook time. Tap for detail: title, servings stepper (adjusts ingredient quantities), ingredients list, and step-by-step instructions. Add/edit recipe sheet: title, servings, ingredients (add/remove rows), instructions (add/remove steps), cook time. Search recipes. Persist with Codable + UserDefaults.
```
