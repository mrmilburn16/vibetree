# Feedback form (Medium)

**Prompt (copy-paste into Vibetree Pro)**

```
Build a feedback form with validation and history. Main screen: multi-step form — Step 1: name and email (validated). Step 2: category picker (Bug, Feature, Other) and message text editor. Step 3: rating (1–5 stars) and optional screenshot placeholder. Submit shows success screen with animation. Feedback history tab: list of past submissions. Persist with Codable + UserDefaults.
```
